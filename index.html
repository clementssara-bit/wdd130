Oregon is known for it's variety of landscapes; from the rugged coastline, temperate rainforests and towering cascade range mountains. The capital of Oregon is Salem, which is in the Willamette Valley. Oregon is also known for Lewis and Clark's expedition from 1804-1806 the Oregon Trail which began in the 1840's.
